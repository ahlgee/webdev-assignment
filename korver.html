Kyle Korver

Kyle Elliot Korver was born on March 17, 1981 is an American professional basketball player. He was traded to Cleveland cavaliers by Atlanta hawks in 2017. He played for the cavs from 2017 up to the present. He is a all star. He won the Nba sportmanship award back in 2015. He also holds a record of the highest three point field goal percentage in Nba
