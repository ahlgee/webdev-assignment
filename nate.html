Nate Thurmond

Nate Thurmond was born on July 25, 1941 is an American retired professional basketball player. He was traded to Cleveland Cavaliers in 1975. He played for the cavs from 1976-1977. He scored 14,437, Rebounds 14,464 Assist 2,575 throughout his career in the NBA. Nate Thurmond died on July 16, 2016 after a short battle with leukemia.
